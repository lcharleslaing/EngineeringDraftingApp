Engineering Review & Release Process

Technical Review

  • Comprehensive review of all engineering drawings
  • Verification of calculations and design assumptions
  • Check for compliance with project specifications
  • Validate design against applicable codes and standards

Compliance Verification

  • Verify adherence to industry standards (ASME, ANSI, etc.)
  • Check compliance with safety regulations
  • Validate environmental and sustainability requirements
  • Ensure quality management system compliance

Peer Review

  • Independent review by senior engineer
  • Cross-check of critical design elements
  • Validation of engineering judgment and decisions
  • Review of risk assessment and mitigation strategies

Final Approval

  • Engineering manager sign-off on design
  • Final verification of all requirements
  • Authorization for manufacturing release
  • Documentation of approval and release decision
