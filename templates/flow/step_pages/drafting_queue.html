  • Create base drawings from specifications
  • Develop 3D models and assemblies
  • Generate detailed component drawings
  • Create assembly and installation drawings

Engineering Calculations

  • Perform structural and mechanical calculations
  • Verify design against applicable codes and standards
  • Calculate loads, stresses, and safety factors
  • Document calculation methods and results

Quality Assurance

  • Internal review of all drawings and calculations
  • Cross-check dimensions and specifications
  • Verify compliance with project requirements
  • Final quality control before release

Documentation Standards

  • Include appropriate notes and specifications
  • Maintain revision control and version management
