Print Review & Approval Guidelines

Review Process

  • Verify print completeness and accuracy
  • Check dimensions and tolerances against specifications
  • Ensure compliance with engineering standards
  • Validate material specifications and requirements
  • Review for manufacturability and cost implications

Quality Standards

  • All dimensions must be clearly defined and measurable
  • Tolerances must be appropriate for manufacturing capabilities
  • Material specifications must be complete and accurate
  • Assembly instructions must be clear and unambiguous

Approval Criteria

  • Technical accuracy and completeness
  • Compliance with project specifications
  • Manufacturability and cost-effectiveness
  • Safety and regulatory compliance
