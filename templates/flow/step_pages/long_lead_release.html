Long Lead Procurement Process

Item Identification

  • Review all prints and specifications for long lead items
  • Identify components with extended manufacturing times
  • Catalog custom or specialized equipment requirements
  • Document critical path dependencies

Vendor Research

  • Identify qualified suppliers for each item
  • Verify vendor capabilities and certifications
  • Obtain preliminary quotes and lead times
  • Assess vendor reliability and quality history

Lead Time Analysis

  • Calculate critical path impact of each item
  • Identify items that could delay project completion
  • Prioritize items based on project timeline
  • Develop contingency plans for critical items

Release Authorization

  • Obtain project manager approval for procurement
  • Verify budget allocation and cost approval
  • Confirm technical specifications are final
  • Document authorization and approval chain
