Purchasing Release Process

Final Verification

  • Verify all engineering requirements are complete
  • Confirm all specifications are final and approved
  • Check that all necessary documentation is included
  • Validate project timeline and delivery requirements

Purchasing Package Preparation

  • Compile all engineering drawings and specifications
  • Include material specifications and requirements
  • Prepare vendor qualification criteria
  • Document quality requirements and inspection criteria

Cost Estimation Review

  • Review preliminary cost estimates
  • Validate budget allocation and approval
  • Check for cost optimization opportunities
  • Confirm funding availability and authorization

Release Authorization

  • Obtain final project manager approval
  • Verify purchasing department readiness
  • Confirm vendor database and qualification status
  • Document release authorization and timeline
